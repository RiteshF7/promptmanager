Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

Prompt Manager runs in the background and listens for your custom shortcuts. When you type a shortcut (e.g., !email), it automatically replaces it with your defined text.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All your prompts are stored locally on your machine. We don't send your keystrokes to any server.

Can I use it for coding?

Absolutely. You can create snippets for code blocks, boilerplate, or documentation templates.
